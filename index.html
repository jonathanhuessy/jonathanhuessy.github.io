About Me

My fascination lies at the intersection of software, mechanics, and mathematics. I'm driven by a deep curiosity to understand how things work and a passion for building systems that perform with precision and reliability. With a Master's degree in Mechanical Engineering from ETH Zurich, specializing in Robotics, I've spent over a decade applying a first-principles approach to solve complex challenges. My journey has taken me from developing life-changing medical devices in Switzerland to engineering guidance systems for space missions here in New Zealand.
